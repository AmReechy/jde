About Us

1. Company Overview

JDE CONSULTE is a simplified joint-stock company (SAS) registered in France under the RCS of Pontoise with company number 980 971 725. Our head office is in 96340, Persan.

2. Our Expertise

We specialize in two main areas of activity (NAF/APE code 74.30Z):

  • Translation and Interpretation Services
  • Administrative Support and Consulting

This dual focus allows us to combine linguistic accuracy with practical assistance in administrative procedures.

3. Legal and Administrative Details

5. Commitment to the Community

Beyond our formal legal registration, we also play an active role in supporting individuals, especially within the Nigerian diaspora in France and abroad. Our services extend to:

  • Assisting with official Nigerian documents (e.g., birth certificates)
  • Helping with legalization and administrative procedures
  • Providing translation and interpretation for cross-border needs
  • Facilitating document processing between France and Nigeria

This community-oriented focus ensures that our clients can rely on trusted, professional, and culturally aware guidance.

6. Our Mission

At JDE CONSULTE, our mission is clear: To simplify administrative and linguistic procedures for individuals and organizations, offering clarity, trust, and professionalism.

We combine:

  • High-quality translation and interpretation
  • Tailored administrative assistance
  • A people-first approach that understands the challenges faced by multicultural communities
