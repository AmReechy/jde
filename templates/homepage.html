How it Works

We have a 4-step approach to providing our services

1

User Registration

As a customer, you would need to register on our website and then be logged in to your account to request any of our services.

2

Service Selection and Payment

Select the service you want us to render, make an appropriate payment for that service, and provide all the relevant information and details required.

3

Process Begins

Once payment for the selected service is received together with all the relevant information, we assign your service request to one of our partners /consultants and work starts

4

Service Rendered

We provide the service results and feedback to you on the stipulated time frame.
